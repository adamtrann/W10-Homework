The air quality plot displays PM2.5 readings (in micrograms per cubic meter) from various monitoring stations in Sydney, including Bargo, Bringelly, Camden, Campbelltown West, Liverpool, and Oakdale, for December 2019. This visualization provides valuable insights into the particulate matter levels across these locations, helping to identify trends and potential areas of concern.

Notably, the plot features a selection on the legend functionality, allowing users to filter the data by specific stations for a more focused analysis of air quality trends in their area. To enable users to make better sense of certain points within the plot, a tool tip feature has also been applied.
